Welcome To Homology

What We Are: a community driven site for depositing images of different types of data that are directly associated with specific taxa and discussions as to hypothesized homology between elements across taxa.
 
Our Goals: Here we hope that the anatomcal, functional anatomical, and systematic communities will engage in providing their expertise in not only the theoretical issues surrounding the topic of homology but will participate in aiding in standardization of names for features under discussion as well as their opinions as to standardized abbreviations for features
 
We also hope that users will contribute photographs, drawings, and any other images or movies that will provide a database for users to compare features hypothesized to be homologous across taxa. These contributions will provide a outstanding database for education and research and permit one to compare features of all types to those at your research bench, in museums, or data collected in previous studies.
